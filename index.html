What's Knit4Good?

As homelessness grows in the country, people effected aren't alloted proper resources to survive. From food to clothes, the needs of homeless people aren't met, leading to them being stuck. But with Knit4Good, it closes the gap and allows for people in need to be warm, secure, and confident to push beyond their circumstances.

Homelessness in the US

According to the US Department of Housing and Urban Development, the amount of chronically homeless people in the US hasn't drastically changed over the past 9 years. Chronic homelessness is defined to be a person homeless for a year or more. The questions needed to be asked is why hasn't the amount go down, and how can the US finally reduce the amount of chronically homeless people? That's where Knit4Wear comes in.

With Knit4 Wear, you'll be able to contribute to the fight against homelessness whilst learning a new hobby!
